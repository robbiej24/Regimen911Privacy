Regimen911

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Regimen911 is built to keep your progress on your device. This policy explains what the app stores, what it does not collect, and how sharing and purchases work.

Overview

Regimen911 is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not create user accounts, and we do not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal information to use the app.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking to monitor you inside or outside of the app.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores your goal settings, daily progress history, reminder preferences, and related app state locally on your device so the app can function offline.

This locally stored information is not sent to our servers because Regimen911 does not operate a backend service for user data.

Sharing

If you choose to use the in-app sharing feature, Regimen911 creates an image of your progress summary on your device and hands it to the iOS share sheet only after you ask it to.

Any app or service you choose from the share sheet, such as Messages or a social media app, handles that shared image under its own privacy practices.

Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or restore purchases, those transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive your full payment card information. Purchase-related data is handled by Apple according to Apple's policies and your App Store account settings.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Regimen911 stores your preferred reminder time on your device and uses local notifications to remind you to log your progress.

Notification permissions can be changed at any time in your device settings.

Data Retention And Deletion

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you control how long it stays there.

If you delete the app, the locally stored tracker history and settings associated with the app are deleted from that device and cannot be recovered by us.

Children

Regimen911 is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Last Updated date shown in the app and on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the support contact information listed on the App Store product page for Regimen911.
