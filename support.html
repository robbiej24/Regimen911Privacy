Regimen911

Support

Regimen911 is designed to be simple, private, and local-first. This page covers the key support information Apple may ask users to access from the App Store listing.

Your Data

Regimen911 stores progress history, settings, and reminder preferences locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not run a backend service for user tracker data.

If you delete the app, the locally stored tracker history and settings associated with the app are deleted from that device and cannot be recovered by us.

Subscriptions

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Subscription billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are managed under Apple's rules and your App Store account settings.

If you restore purchases on a supported iOS build, the app will refresh access from Apple's purchase records.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Regimen911 stores your preferred reminder time on your device and uses local notifications to remind you to log your progress.

Privacy

Regimen911 does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking. You can review the full privacy policy at the public privacy policy page.

Contact

For help or questions, use the support contact information listed on the App Store product page for Regimen911.
